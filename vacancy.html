Description of the vacancy

CORA Homes is looking for a Senior/Middle 3D Artist to work on international architectural projects. Work remotely. We need a CGI ninja who will create exterior design visualization, interior design and 3D animation.

Job Responsibilities:

1. Ability to work with V-Ray, Corona

2. Ability to read blueprints

3. Attention to detail

4. Stress resistance

5. Desire to learn

6. Experience working under deadlines

7. Knowledge of ZBrush, Marvelous Designer, Substance Painter will be an advantage.

8. Experience in communication with a costumers.

Qualifications:

1. High level of proficiency in 3ds Max and Photoshop

2. Finished 3D Artist courses

3. Engineering and technical thinking, understanding of physics, mechanics, enthusiasm for technology (most tasks are related to the modeling of technology)
